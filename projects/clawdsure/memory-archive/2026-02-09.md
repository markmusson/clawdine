### The Product

**ClawdSure = Observability-based insurance for AI agents**

Motor insurance parallel (but reframed):
- Black box recorder in car → Observability instrumentation on machine
- Driving data → Security telemetry (config drift, audit results, chain integrity)
- Good driving = lower premium → Clean audit history = valid coverage
- Crash assessed against driving data → Breach assessed against attestation chain

**Positioning shift:** "Telematics" is car-specific. "Observability" speaks to developers, works for any instrumented system (agents, trading bots, infra automation), and aligns with mature tooling (DataDog, OTEL, Prometheus). **Observability-as-underwriting.**

**Economics:**
- $50 premium → $500 payout (Basic tier)
- Target loss ratio: 55%
- USDT premiums routed to Morpho vaults via HyperSend
- 6-12% APY on float (vs 3-5% traditional)
- Yield adds $2.25/policy (4.5% of premium)
- Combined ratio: 85% with yield

**TAM:**
- 2M OpenClaw agents by 2026
- 10M+ autonomous agents by 2028
- $100M-$250M premium potential at full penetration
- Year 1 target: 50K policies = $2.5M GWP
- Year 3 target: 500K policies = $25M GWP

**The Wedge:**
OpenClaw is the first viral personal AI agent framework and the most unsecured. If we can make OpenClaw instances insurable, we prove the model. Then expand to Langchain, Vercel AI SDK, etc.
